About SIH 2025

Smart India Hackathon (SIH) is a nationwide initiative by the Ministry of Education's Innovation Cell to provide students a platform to solve some of the pressing problems we face in our daily lives, and thus inculcate a culture of product innovation and a mindset of problem-solving. This internal hackathon, brought to you by SDC and SparshNFC, is your first step towards the national stage.

Innovation

Bring your most creative and innovative ideas to life. Develop unique solutions to real-world challenges and showcase your technical prowess.

Collaboration

Work in a team of talented individuals. Learn from your peers, share knowledge, and build something amazing together in a high-energy environment.

Opportunity

Winners not only get amazing prizes but also the chance to represent our institution at the national level Smart India Hackathon, opening doors to mentorship and career opportunities.

Themes of SIH 2025

Here is a selection of problem statements from various themes. Teams are encouraged to explore the full list on the official SIH portal and choose a problem that aligns with their skills and passion.

Healthcare

AI for Diabetic Retinopathy Detection

Develop an AI model to analyze retinal fundus images for early signs of diabetic retinopathy, providing a severity score to assist doctors in remote areas.

Ministry of Health and Family Welfare

Agriculture

Smart Irrigation System using IoT

Create an affordable IoT-based system that monitors soil moisture, weather forecasts, and crop type to automate irrigation, conserving water.

Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare

Education

Platform for Vernacular Languages

Build a platform that provides quality technical education and resources in various Indian vernacular languages to bridge the language barrier.

Ministry of Education

Smart City

Real-time Public Transport Tracking

Develop a system for tracking public transport (buses, trains) in real-time, providing accurate arrival times and route information to commuters.

Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs

Fintech

Blockchain-based Digital Certificate Verification

Create a secure system using blockchain to issue and verify academic and professional certificates, preventing fraud and forgery.

Ministry of Finance

Cybersecurity

Phishing Detection using Machine Learning

Build a browser extension or tool that uses ML to detect and warn users about potential phishing websites and emails in real-time.

Ministry of Home Affairs
